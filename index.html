TTH: Text-to-Talking-Head

Text-to-Talking-Head is the first text-driven talking head generator that maintains a stable viewing perspective. It does this by fusing a TTS model with a Diffusion model such that their latents are used to drive the other

Stable Perspective

TTH puts emphasis on producing videos with a stable viewing perspective

Flexible Driving Input

Using text as the driving input allows for faster generation and editing of the driving inputs

Furthermore it allows for adaptive voice generation.
